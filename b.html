Extra Useful Tips

Hydration is Key

Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily. Muscle recovery and performance depend heavily on your hydration levels.

Prioritize Sleep

Muscles grow when you rest, not when you lift. Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep every night.

Protein Intake

Consume roughly 1.6g to 2g of protein per kg of body weight to support muscle synthesis and repair.
